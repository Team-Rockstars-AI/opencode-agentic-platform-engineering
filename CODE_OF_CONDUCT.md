# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, welcoming, and learning-oriented
environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in
this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind, respectful, and patient — especially with colleagues who are learning.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive, teaching-oriented feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the team and the project.
- Assuming good intent and asking clarifying questions before reacting.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Dismissive or belittling responses to questions, especially from newcomers.
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Our responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behaviour
and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to
any instances of unacceptable behaviour.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned
with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — issues, pull requests,
discussions, and reviews — and when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the maintainers through your internal Team Rockstars AI channel. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
